Initial User Research

Qualitative User Research is the name of the game in a Psych Degree


For user research, given that this was a skills learning oriented project, it was important to do a variety of types of research, but also choose the methods that were most relevant to the goals. In order to keep users at the center of the project, the first step was to conduct some interviews to see if there are any hidden dimensions to the project, and whether the problem that I noticed was truly a trending opportunity, rather than perception.


Once I had enough leads to explore, I accumulated all the data and then organized it into piles of relevance. In retrospect, the ideas could use better defined categories, as that might have made a stronger impact on the directions that I wanted to take this app along the way. Admittedly, some of the context has been lost over time. I should make sure the next time I do this that it is fully contextualized when I am done with it, and probably buy a magnet board too. Red was negative, Green was positive, and Blue represents some concept about the interactions that I would be dealing with.
