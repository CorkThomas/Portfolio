Usability Testing

Users are our best source of feedback.


I also conducted a preference test between two possible onboarding screens. The information was helpful for deciding which direction I want to go, but admittedly the choices were not all that different. The most important part of this was to know how to conduct those tests.


I used Usability Hub for this purpose. The website not only was easy for me to use, it also let me know when there was a significant result, so I didn't have to do the math based on the responses that I had to assert significance.

Overall, people prefered the lighter color and friendlier font. The whole process was simple for me and easy to recruit people for, especially given the short duration of the test.
