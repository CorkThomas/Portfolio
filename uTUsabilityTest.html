About Me

Usability Testing

Users are our best source of feedback.


I did a round of usability testing, which was useful for gathering information about what users liked, disliked, and how they felt about all the features of the design that were already in place.


From there, I had to alternate between doing research and applying changes to the design based on the user feedback that I was collecting.


I set up a simulation on my computer and recorded users while they were interacting with the design. I prepared a script that included privacy and questions about the design and how they felt about it. The questions ranged from open-ended questions to direct tasks for participants to follow.
