Initial User Research

Qualitative research is the name of the game for building empathy towards the user.


Once the personas were well enough defined, I started to think about what actions and processes they might find to be most useful or essential to the app itself. I started to draw up some possible core processes, and user flows.


This research allowed me to paint a decent picture so that I could choose which direction I wanted to push the overall strategy of the design. What I didn't realize at this point was that the strategy itself changes quite a bit throughout the entire process, as we come to realize which parts are most important.
