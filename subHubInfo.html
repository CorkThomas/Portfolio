SubHub Premise




SubHub is a subscription manager that is meant to give users maximum awareness over their monthly spending.


Each subscription is represented by a card that users can use to manage, visualize, and unsubscribe from the service or goods.





SubHub also includes a store for people to browse new monthly or yearly subscriptions that might interest them.



Subscribing is as easy as unsubscribing, and total monthly spending is always available to plainly see.







Our biggest concern is awareness, so we designed a notification system that alerts you about upcoming charges, rising costs, and subscriptions that are near the end of their limited cycle.




We made sure to highlight the important information.





The profile page lets you see the key info in one place, as well as change some settings.



We made sure to include an emergency mass-unsubscription option.
