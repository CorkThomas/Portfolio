Initial User Research

Qualitative research is the name of the game for building empathy towards the user.


For user research, given that this was a skills learning oriented project, it was important to do a variety of types of research, but also to choose the methods that were most relevant to the goals. In order to keep users at the center of the project, the first step was to conduct some interviews to see if there are any hidden dimensions to the project, and whether the problem that I noticed was truly a trending opportunity for a new useful too, rather than my own perception of an imaginary problem.
