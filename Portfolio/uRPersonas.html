Initial User Research

Qualitative User Research is the name of the game in a Psych Degree


After a round of qualitative research, I compiled all the data into three proto-personas, three fictional characters that would represent the people who I was designing for. From there I started fleshing them out and verifying my claims about these personas with academic research.
I then organized the data into cards, for reference.
